Base style (μπαίνει σε ΚΑΘΕ prompt)

Style tag
warm children's picture-book illustration in a simple FLAT storybook style (NOT semi-realistic, NOT glossy editorial illustration, NOT detailed digital painting, NOT textured/grainy illustration, NO paper or canvas grain texture) — soft rounded shapes, thick clean uniform linework in deep indigo (#2E2A47, NOT black), flat single-tone gouache/watercolor color fills with minimal to no shading (no fabric folds, no directional gradients), big expressive eyes, pastel palette (lavender #7B6FC4, coral #E85D42, sunny yellow #F4B93C, sage green #5AA588, soft blue #4E85B8), simple toddler-book proportions, warm soft flat lighting, no photorealism, no anime style, no semi-realistic rendering, no gradient shading, no jewelry, no detailed fabric texture
Consistency clause
Maintain exact character consistency across every illustration: same face, hairstyle, proportions and clothing for every recurring character throughout the entire book. Melina = 5.5-year-old girl, brown bob haircut with soft front fringe, pink bow on the side, big brown eyes, light freckles across the nose, denim overall dungarees over a yellow-and-white striped shirt, pink sneakers. Biscuit = small cream/white fluffy puppy, floppy brown ears, blue collar. Mom = brown hair in a ponytail, soft lavender cardigan. Dad = short curly hair, glasses, plaid shirt. Grandma = silver hair in a bun, floral-print apron. Grandpa = white moustache, brimmed hat, plaid vest. Sofia = curly red hair, girl, same age as Melina. Nikos = energetic boy, about 6 years old. Leonidas = quiet boy, about 5 years old, neighbor.
Negative prompt (μπαίνει σε ΚΑΘΕ εικόνα)
photorealism, semi-realistic rendering, glossy/detailed digital painting, paper grain, canvas texture, crayon texture, anime/manga style, scary or dark imagery, text or letters in image, extra limbs or distorted hands/faces, exaggerated adult body proportions, jewelry, detailed fabric texture, fabric folds, heavy gradient shading, blurry, logos or watermarks, violence, clinical or medical imagery

Μεγέθη: Full page (3:4, ολοσέλιδη) · Half page (4:3, μοιράζεται με κείμενο) · Spot (4:3, μικρή)
Priority: HIGH = απαραίτητη πρώτα · MEDIUM = σημαντική · LOW = προαιρετική/διακοσμητική, μπορεί να μπει τελευταία ή να παραλειφθεί αν λείψει χρόνος
